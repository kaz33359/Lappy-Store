Microsoft Certified Refurbisher

A Microsoft Certified Refurbisher (MCR) is a company that refurbishes used computers and laptops, installs genuine Microsoft software, and resells them with a warranty. The MCR program ensures that the refurbished devices meet the high standards of quality, reliability, and authenticity set by Microsoft. This program not only provides affordable access to technology for individuals and organizations but also helps to reduce e-waste and promote environmental sustainability by extending the lifespan of electronic devices.
